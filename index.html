Areas of Research

These are some possible areas of resarch for Machine Learning

  • Healthcare and Life Science

    ML can allow machine learning experts to analyse data for clinitians and medical researchers.

  • Education

    ML can track student knowledge to improvde the performance of teachers and schools.

  • Public Services

    ML in public sectors can be applied in fraud detection, automatitation of routine administration systems or monitoring regulatory systems.

  • Transport and Logistic

    ML can help users to track congestions and save drivers time. Build behavior models to allow insurance companies to set insurance rates for the way users' drive.

  • Finances

    ML can be applicable in risk management, fraud prevention or sentiment analysis.

  • Smart Cities / Internet of Things

    ML and sensors can predict accidents, crimes or save energy cosumption.
